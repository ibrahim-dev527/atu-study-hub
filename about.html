The Man Behind the Mission

Bafiku Emmanuel

Bafiku Emmanuel is a driven student leader at Accra Technical University (ATU) whose commitment to student welfare has defined his time on campus. Known for his warm personality, sharp intellect, and unshakeable dedication to fellow students, Bafiku has consistently shown up when it matters most.

His journey as a student advocate began in his first year when he noticed the significant gap in access to academic resources — specifically past questions — among students from all departments. While others complained, Bafiku acted: he began collecting, organizing, and sharing past questions with classmates, laying the foundation for what would become ATU Study Hub.

Running for SRC President in 2026 under the hashtag #BringBafikusBack, he brings a proven track record of action over promises. His campaign pillars — Empowering Students, Amplifying Voices, and Sankofa — reflect his deep commitment to both academic excellence and student representation.

Meet the Developer

Ibrahim Mohammed Lotsu

Full-Stack Web Developer & UI/UX Designer

Ibrahim Mohammed Lotsu, known professionally as Ibratech, is a talented full-stack web developer and digital creative based in Ghana. He designed and built the entire ATU Study Hub platform from the ground up — including all 12 pages, the Supabase backend integration, the image swiper, live search functionality, WhatsApp integrations, and the admin dashboard.

Passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems, Ibrahim partnered with Bafiku Emmanuel to build a platform that puts academic resources directly in the hands of every ATU student — for free. He believes good design and clean code can change communities.
