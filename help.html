Help Center

Everything you need to know about using ATU Study Hub. Find guides, FAQs, and direct support options below.

How-To Guides

Step-by-Step Guides

📥
How to Download Past Questions
  1. 1Go to the Past Questions page from the navigation menu
  2. 2Use the search bar or filters to find your course
  3. 3Find the past question card you need
  4. 4Click the red "Download" button on the card
  5. 5The PDF will download automatically to your device
🔍
How to Search and Filter
  1. 1Go to the Past Questions page
  2. 2Type a course name or keyword in the search bar
  3. 3Use the Faculty dropdown to narrow by faculty
  4. 4Use Level and Year filters to refine further
  5. 5Results update instantly — click Reset to clear filters
👤
How to Create an Account
  1. 1Click "Login / Signup" in the top navigation
  2. 2Click "Create Account" or go to signup.html
  3. 3Fill in your full name, ATU email, phone, and password
  4. 4Click "Create Account" — you'll get a confirmation
  5. 5Log in and access your personalized dashboard
FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ATU Study Hub completely free? ▼
Yes! ATU Study Hub is 100% free for all ATU students. Creating an account is free, downloading past questions is free, and it will always remain free. Bafiku's mission is to ensure no student is left behind due to lack of resources.
Do I need an account to download past questions? ▼
You can browse past questions without an account, but creating a free account gives you additional benefits like saving your favourites, tracking your download history, and receiving notifications when new past questions are added for your department.
What if I can't find past questions for my course? ▼
If you can't find a specific past question, you can request it directly via WhatsApp. Message Bafiku on 053 680 1965 or 054 482 3484 with your course name, faculty, department, level, and year — and we'll upload it within 24 hours!
Can I upload past questions to help other students? ▼
Absolutely! Sharing is what makes this community strong. You can upload past questions through the Dashboard (after logging in). All uploads go through a quick review by the admin team to ensure quality before being published.
Are the past questions from all ATU faculties? ▼
Yes! We cover all 6 major faculties at ATU: Engineering, Business & Management, Computing & Information Systems, Health Sciences, Art & Design, and Applied Sciences. With over 20 departments covered, we're adding more every week.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▼
Go to the Login page and click "Forgot Password?" Enter your registered email address and you'll receive a password reset link. If you're still having trouble, contact us on WhatsApp at 053 680 1965 for direct assistance.
Are the past questions authentic ATU exam papers? ▼
Yes! All past questions are sourced from actual ATU examinations and are verified by students who have sat those exams. We do not upload mock questions or questions from other institutions. Quality and authenticity are our top priority.
How does this relate to Bafiku's campaign? ▼
ATU Study Hub is one of the most visible proof-points of what Bafiku Emmanuel promises as SRC President: real action, not just words. He built this platform before getting elected to show what he's capable of delivering when given the full mandate. The platform will continue regardless of election outcomes.
